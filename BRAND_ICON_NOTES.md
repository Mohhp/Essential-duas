# Brand-Faithful Icon Notes

This icon direction is based on the app's shipped visual system rather than the earlier concept prompt.

## Source Of Truth In The App

- Background family: deep navy, not emerald-first
- Primary surfaces: `#0a1628`, `#0f1d30`, `#050b14`
- Accent gold: `#C9A84C`
- Accent green: `#2ECC71`
- Branding motif: simple crescent mark with modern typography and restrained glow
- Splash/logo treatment: gold crescent on dark background, with green/gold accents and minimal ornament

## What This Variant Tries To Match

- The dark navy atmosphere from the live app and splash screen
- The gold crescent as the recognizable brand anchor
- The green accent as a secondary highlight, not the main background color
- The simple monogram feel from the existing logo system rather than ornate calligraphy
