## Full Description (English, 4000 chars max)

**Falah** — حيّ على الفلاح — is a beautifully crafted Islamic companion app for
Afghan and Pashto-speaking Muslims. Everything works offline, once installed.

---

### 🤲 60+ Essential Duas
Curated duas organised into 12 thematic sections:
- Quranic Foundation
- Protection & Refuge
- Forgiveness & Repentance
- Guidance & Steadfastness  
- Wellbeing & Health
- Morning & Evening Adhkar
- Provision & Sustenance
- Prayer & Worship
- Travel
- From the Scholars
- Ramadan
- Evil Eye & Magic

Each dua shows the Arabic text, transliteration, and English meaning. Mark
any dua as a favourite for instant access later.

---

### 📖 Quran — Juz 30 with Pashto Audio
Read and listen to Juz 30 (the last juz — Surah An-Naba to An-Nas). Audio is
recited in Pashto by Sheikh Zakaria — ideal for Afghan learners who want to
understand the Quran in their own language. Track your reading with a daily
streak so the habit sticks.

---

### 🕌 Prayer Times — All 5 Prayers
Accurate prayer times calculated for your exact location (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr,
Maghrib, Isha). The home screen shows a live countdown to the next prayer.

---

### 📿 Tasbeeh Counter
A clean digital counter for dhikr and tasbih. Tap to count — no fuss.

---

### 🧭 Qibla Compass
Points toward the Kaaba using your device's compass.

---

### 📜 40 Hadith of Imam an-Nawawi
The complete Arba'in collection — 40 selected hadiths that every Muslim
should know, presented in a readable format.

---

### ⭐ Favourites & Daily Hadith
- Save any dua to your favourites for quick access
- A fresh Hadith of the Day appears on the home screen each day
- See your dua progress summary at a glance

---

### ✈️ Fully Offline
Install once — no internet needed. Works anywhere: on a plane, in a remote
village, wherever you are.
