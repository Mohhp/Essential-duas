# Play Store Icon Brief — Falah

## Brand Snapshot

- App name: Falah — فلاح
- Meaning: success, flourishing, coming to success
- Positioning: premium Islamic worship companion, not only a dua app
- Core functions: authenticated duas, Quran access, prayer times, Qibla, tasbeeh, memorization, Pashto support, reminders, offline use
- Tone: calm, serene, sacred, trustworthy, premium, modern

## Visual Direction

- Primary mood: luxury brand restraint with spiritual warmth
- Maximum three elements only: Arabic calligraphy, crescent, minimal open Quran silhouette
- Preferred palette:
  - background emerald to near-black green: `#0A3D2A`, `#0B1F17`
  - antique gold accent: `#C9A84C`, `#D4AF37`
  - luminous calligraphy white: `#FFF8E7`
  - understated book accent: muted gold or soft white at low opacity

## Composition

- Format: 1024x1024 square canvas with all important content inside the center 80%
- First read: the Arabic word `فلاح` in elegant calligraphy, dominant and centered
- Second read: a refined gold crescent cradling or illuminating the calligraphy
- Third read: a minimal open Quran or book silhouette beneath the calligraphy as a quiet base
- Background: subtle emerald gradient with gentle depth, not glossy and not flat
- Lighting: warm crescent-sourced glow softly illuminating the text, with a sacred halo feel

## Strictly Avoid

- English text or Latin letters
- Stars
- Mosque silhouettes, domes, or minarets
- Busy ornament, arabesques, or geometric pattern fills
- Cartoon illustration, plastic gradients, neon colors, red, or blue
- Clutter or more than three meaningful visual elements

## What The Icon Should Communicate

- Immediate Islamic identity through refined calligraphy rather than clichés
- Peace, devotion, quiet confidence, and sacred value
- Premium mobile app quality suitable for top-tier App Store and Play Store presentation
- Strong legibility at both 1024px and very small launcher sizes

## Prompt You Can Reuse Elsewhere

Design a premium mobile app icon for an Islamic worship companion app called Falah. Use the Arabic calligraphy `فلاح` as the central focal point and make it the first thing the eye reads. A refined antique-gold crescent should cradle or illuminate the calligraphy, acting as the source of a soft divine glow. Beneath it, include only a very minimal open Quran or book silhouette as a subtle base. Use a deep emerald to near-black green background with a very restrained gradient, luminous warm white calligraphy, and refined gold lighting accents. Keep the composition perfectly centered, spacious, and balanced with only three visual elements total: calligraphy, crescent, and book. Avoid all English text, stars, mosque silhouettes, busy decoration, cartoon styling, cheap gradients, and extra colors. The result should feel sacred, serene, luxurious, modern, and highly legible at both 1024px and small mobile icon sizes.
