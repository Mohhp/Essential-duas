Privacy Policy

Essential Duas by فلاح — The Crown Collection

Overview

Essential Duas ("the App") is a free Islamic dua and dhikr application created by Engineer Mohammad Falah. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

  • No account required — You do not need to sign up or log in.
  • No personal information collected — We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any other personal identifier.
  • No analytics or tracking — The App does not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any third-party tracking tools.
  • No advertising — The App contains no ads and no ad-tracking SDKs.

Local Storage

The App stores your preferences and progress locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Bookmarked duas
  • Read/unread status
  • Tasbeeh counter values
  • Daily routine progress
  • Memorization progress (spaced repetition data)
  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Font size settings
  • Language preference

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any cloud service or shared with anyone. Clearing your browser data will remove this stored information.

Location Data

The App may request access to your device's location only for the following features:

  • Prayer Times — To calculate accurate prayer times for your location.
  • Qibla Direction — To show the direction of the Qibla from your current position.

Location data is processed entirely on your device. It is not sent to any server or stored beyond the current session. You can deny location access and the App will continue to function — you can manually enter your city instead.

Audio / Speech

The App uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine to recite duas aloud. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The App loads the following resources from third-party CDNs:

  • Google Fonts — For Arabic and display typography (Amiri, Noto Naskh Arabic, Cinzel, Playfair Display). Google's privacy policy applies to font loading.
  • jsDelivr CDN — For the Adhan.js prayer time library and html2canvas. These are open-source libraries loaded client-side.

No user data is shared with these services.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. It does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
